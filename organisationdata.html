DATA Assessment

Evaluate your supply chain digital transformation and unlock higher performance.


Why DATA Assessment?

Digital transformation in the supply chain enables organizations to gain complete visibility across operations, improving planning, decision-making, and responsiveness.

The goal is to optimize existing resources while achieving higher business performance and sustainability.

Who Should Take It?

  • Organizations using legacy systems
  • Companies lacking digital integration
  • Businesses seeking operational improvement
  • Students and professionals studying supply chain

How to Use Your DATA Score

Your DATA Score helps you:

  • Identify system strengths and weaknesses
  • Benchmark digital maturity
  • Improve supply chain processes
  • Track improvement over time

How to Check Your DATA Score

Complete the assessment to determine your digital readiness and sustainability level. Based on your score, you can take targeted action for improvement.
