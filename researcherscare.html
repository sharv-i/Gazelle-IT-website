Methodology of SCARE Assessment

The SCARE Assessment is designed not only for businesses but also for researchers and students exploring supply chain sustainability. It applies a Weighted Average Model evaluating both Process and Technology maturity across 21 Supply Chain Functional Domains.

The framework prioritizes domains critical to sustainability, efficiency, and risk mitigation, ensuring measurable and actionable results.

SCARE Reports

SCARE reports provide structured analysis of technology adoption across supply chain domains. Technologies evaluated include:

  • Blockchain Integration
  • IoT & Smart Sensors
  • Digital Analytics
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Supply Chain Coordination Platforms
Reports identify which technologies can be leveraged within specific functional domains for maximum impact.
