What is DATA?

The DATA Score refers to the digital transformation maturity of every element of your supply chain. This assessment helps companies improve planning, decision-making, and responsiveness, with the ultimate goal of optimizing resources and achieving higher business performance.

How We Developed the Assessment

Gazelle conducted extensive research across 20+ industries and 670+ companies in 21 functional domains to build a reliable DATA assessment framework. The evaluation tracks your supply chain across these domains and identifies areas requiring specific focus and improvement.

How to Read Your DATA Score

After completing the assessment, your DATA score will position you on the DATA ladder. A higher score indicates a more digitally mature, sustainable, and efficient supply chain. Lower scores highlight opportunities for continuous improvement.

DATA Ladder

The DATA ladder shows where your business stands in terms of digital supply chain maturity. Unsatisfactory scores pinpoint problem areas and provide guidance to improve processes, technology adoption, and overall efficiency.

By leveraging the ladder insights, you can make targeted interventions to achieve sustainable, high-performing supply chains.
