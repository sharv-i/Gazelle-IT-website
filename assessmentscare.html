Identify common problem statements in your supply chain systems and evaluate the technologies and processes you follow across 21 functional domains. This preparation ensures accurate SCARE results and actionable insights.

How to Read Your SCARE Score

SCARE Scores range from 1-10. A high score indicates a more sustainable and efficient supply chain. Scores below 7 signify opportunities for continuous improvement and targeted interventions.

Assessment Process

The SCARE assessment evaluates your supply chain technologies and processes across 21 functional domains. A weighted average approach based on your responses to critical questions produces a final score, which positions your organization on the SCARE Ladder.

This helps prioritize improvement areas and strengthens overall supply chain sustainability and efficiency.
