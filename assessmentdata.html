Prepare for Your DATA Assessment

Before DATA Assessment

Before performing a DATA assessment, you should clearly understand the common problem statements within your supply chain system.

Identify the technologies and processes currently used across functional domains of your supply chain. This preparation ensures you are ready for a meaningful evaluation.

How to Read Your DATA Score

The DATA Score ranges from 1 to 10.

  • 8–10: Highly sustainable and efficient supply chain
  • 7: Stable but requires monitoring
  • Below 7: Significant scope for continuous improvement

A higher DATA Score indicates stronger digital maturity and operational excellence.

Assessment Process

The evaluation identifies supply chain technologies and processes across 21 functional domains.

A weighted average scoring model forms the foundation of your final DATA Score, based on responses to critical supply chain questions.

The final score determines your placement in DATA rankings.
