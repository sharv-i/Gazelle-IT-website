Understanding the SCARE Score

The SCARE Score evaluates your supply chain sustainability and efficiency across multiple functional domains. It identifies risks, highlights improvement areas, and supports cost optimization through measurable performance insights.

How We Developed the Assessment

Gazelle conducted extensive research across 20+ industries and 670+ businesses spanning 21 supply chain functional domains. This comprehensive study enabled us to build a structured assessment framework for organizations seeking measurable supply chain improvements.
